A simple TEXAS HOLD'EM poker hand ranking service.
Usage:
The API expects the following URL build:
/hand/board
Card format has to follow the conventional poker hand formatting rules:
Ace of spades : As
King of hearts : Kh
Ten of clubs : Tc
Deuce of diamonds : 2d
hand has to be exactly 2 cards long(4 characters)
board can be 3 to 5 cards long(6 to 10 characters)
example:
'/AcKs/As2c3c4c8d'
response is in json:
{
"rank": "Pair",
"strength": 3363
}
strength is valued on a scale of 1 to 7462, where 1 is a Royal Flush
and 7462 is unsuited 7-5-4-3-2, as there are only 7642 distinctly ranked hands in poker.
